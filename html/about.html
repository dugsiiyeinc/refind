About Lost & Found

Connecting People with Their Belongings

Refind is more than just a platform—it's a community-driven movement dedicated to reuniting people with their lost belongings and spreading kindness one item at a time.

Our Vision

A World Where Nothing Stays Lost

We envision a world where losing something doesn't mean it's gone forever. Through technology and community collaboration, we're creating a global network where every lost item has a chance to be reunited with its owner.

Our vision extends beyond just finding lost items—we're building a culture of trust, kindness, and mutual support where people look out for one another and celebrate small acts of goodness.

"Every lost item deserves a second chance to find its way home."

"Empowering communities to help each other, one item at a time."

Our Mission

Making Lost Items Found Again

Our mission is to create a simple, effective, and free platform that connects people who have lost items with those who have found them. We're committed to making the reunion process as seamless and stress-free as possible.

Our Story

Refind was born from a simple observation: every day, thousands of items are lost and found, but there's no easy way to connect the two. Whether it's a forgotten phone on a bus, a misplaced wallet at a cafe, or a beloved piece of jewelry dropped in a park, these items hold value—both monetary and sentimental.

We created this platform to bridge that gap. By providing a centralized, easy-to-use system where people can report lost and found items, we're making it possible for these valuable possessions to find their way back home.

What started as a small community project has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of users. Every successful reunion reminds us why we do this work—because behind every lost item is a person hoping to find it, and behind every found item is an opportunity for kindness.
